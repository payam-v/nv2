Our Programs

At Novel Vibrations, we offer a range of programs designed to support healing, skill development, and personal growth through creative expression. Our programs are free and open to adult individuals experiencing homelessness or mental health challenges in the Atlanta area.

Art Therapy Workshops

Our core program offers regular art therapy sessions led by trained art therapists. Participants can explore various artistic mediums, including:

  • Painting (acrylic, watercolor, oil)
  • Drawing (pencil, charcoal, pastel)
  • Sculpture
  • Mixed media and collage
  • Digital art
  • Photography

These workshops provide a safe, non-judgmental space for self-expression, emotional processing, and skill development. No prior artistic experience is necessary.

Entrepreneurship Training

We believe in empowering our participants to turn their creativity into sustainable income. Our entrepreneurship program covers essential business skills, including:

  • Pricing and marketing artwork
  • Basic financial management
  • Customer service and communication
  • Online sales and social media promotion
  • Participating in art markets and exhibitions

Art Exhibitions and Sales

We regularly organize exhibitions to showcase our participants' artwork. These events provide opportunities for:

  • Public recognition of our artists' talents
  • Sales of artwork, with proceeds going directly to the artists
  • Community engagement and awareness-raising

Peer Support Groups

We facilitate peer support groups where participants can share their experiences, challenges, and successes in a supportive environment. These groups help build community and reduce isolation.

Collaborative Art Projects

We organize group projects that allow participants to work together on larger-scale artworks. These collaborations foster teamwork, communication skills, and a sense of shared accomplishment.

Community Outreach and Education

We engage with the broader Atlanta community through:

  • Public workshops and demonstrations
  • Partnerships with local schools and organizations
  • Awareness campaigns about mental health and homelessness

Support Services

While our focus is on creative expression, we recognize that our participants often face multiple challenges. We provide referrals and connections to partner organizations for additional support, including:

  • Housing assistance
  • Mental health services
  • Substance abuse treatment
  • Job training and placement
  • Legal aid

To learn more about our programs or to get involved, please contact us.
