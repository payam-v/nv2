Get Involved

There are many ways to support Novel Vibrations and make a difference in the lives of those we serve. Whether you're an individual or a corporation, we welcome your involvement!

Corporate Partnerships

Novel Vibrations offers unique opportunities for corporations to engage with our mission and create meaningful impact. By partnering with us, your company can:

  • Demonstrate corporate social responsibility
  • Enhance employee engagement and morale
  • Gain positive brand exposure
  • Make a tangible difference in the community
  • Support mental health awareness and destigmatization efforts

Partnership Opportunities

  • Sponsorship: Support our programs, events, or exhibitions through financial contributions or in-kind donations.
  • Employee Engagement: Organize team-building volunteer days or art workshops for your employees.
  • Skills-Based Volunteering: Offer your company's expertise in areas such as marketing, finance, or technology to support our organization.
  • Art in the Workplace: Participate in our corporate art rental program or commission artwork for your office spaces.
  • Matching Gifts: Amplify your employees' charitable contributions through a matching gifts program.
  • Cause Marketing: Align your brand with our mission through co-branded campaigns or product collaborations.

To discuss partnership opportunities tailored to your company's goals and values, please contact us.

Individual Involvement

Volunteer

We rely on dedicated volunteers to help run our programs and support our participants. Volunteer opportunities include:

  • Assisting in art as therapy workshops
  • Helping with exhibition setup and gallery management
  • Providing administrative support
  • Offering professional skills (e.g., legal, marketing, IT)
  • Mentoring participants in entrepreneurship

To become a volunteer, please fill out our Volunteer Application Form.

Donate

Your financial support helps us provide free programs, materials, and resources to our participants. As a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization, all donations to Novel Vibrations are tax-deductible to the full extent allowed by law.

Ways to donate:

  • One-time donation
  • Monthly recurring donation
  • Planned giving
Donate Now

Donate Supplies

We always welcome donations of art supplies and materials. Some items we frequently need include:

  • Paints (acrylic, watercolor, oil)
  • Brushes
  • Canvas and paper
  • Drawing materials (pencils, charcoal, pastels)
  • Sculpting supplies
  • Craft materials

Please contact us to arrange a supply donation.

Attend Our Events

Support our mission by attending our art exhibitions, workshops, and fundraising events. It's a great way to meet our artists, learn about our programs, and potentially purchase artwork.

Spread the Word

Help us raise awareness about Novel Vibrations and the power of creative expression in healing and personal growth:

  • Follow us on social media and share our posts
  • Tell your friends, family, and colleagues about our work
  • Invite us to speak at your community group or workplace

Purchase Artwork

By purchasing artwork created by our participants, you not only acquire a unique piece of art but also directly support the artist and our programs. Visit our gallery or online store to browse available works.

However you choose to get involved, your support makes a real difference in the lives of individuals facing mental health challenges and homelessness. Together, we can create a more compassionate and inclusive community through the power of creativity.

For more information on any of these opportunities, please contact us. We look forward to welcoming you to the Novel Vibrations community!
